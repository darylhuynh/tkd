# Team Dynamics

Baseline interactions, relationships, and group voice for **Kwon's Taekwondo Academy**.

---

## Core Principle

> The team itself should feel like a **family**. The true enemies are **outside** the school.

Internal conflict = friendly competition (Ethan/Kian), not betrayal.

## Relationship Map

### Ethan ↔ Kian
- Friendly internal rivalry
- Not hatred, not betrayal, not enemies
- Constantly push each other to improve
- Same arrival window at Kwon's

### Ethan ↔ Logan
- Brothers with opposite personalities
- Logan pulls Ethan out of isolation

### TJ ↔ Everyone
- Mentor-like figure despite being a peer
- Current team ace — athlete everyone respects
- Logan treats him like an older brother

### Kieryn ↔ Team
- Constant sarcasm and reality checks
- Living in older brother's shadow — may snap at team, never maliciously

### Ariana ↔ Team
- Quiet observer
- Speaks little; actions do the talking
- Trains through conversations

---

## Training Scene Formula

1. **Warm banter** — establish personalities
2. **Drill rotation** — Ethan vs Kian, TJ holding pads, Ariana extra reps
3. **MV observation** — notes for later eval
4. **Fade or cut** — often to Prime flashback (cold palette shift)

---

## Conflict Sources (External)

- Rival schools
- Regional and national competitors
- Tournament brackets
- Team championships
- Prime Taekwondo's reputation warfare
