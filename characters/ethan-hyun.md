## Combat Identity

- Elite technique and endurance
- Counter-fighter — reads opponents, punishes mistakes
- **Berserker Rage:** Unlocked after **grandmother's death** (Arc 5). Triggers on critical hits or extreme training pressure.

**While active:**
- Attack, speed, mobility spike
- Defense drops — Ethan disregards guard
- Kicks hit harder and faster — reckless forward pressure
- Growth arc: recognize trigger, choose control; rage can cost matches at elite level

### Defining Skills

- **Neverending Endurance** — outlasts opponents in extended exchanges
- **Berserker Rage** — grief-fueled mode; post–Arc 5 Ch 58 (see [STORY-OUTCOMES.md](../STORY-OUTCOMES.md))
- **Ultimate Counter** — unlocked **Arc 7 Ch 92** (U20 camp). Reads any committed attack; answers with **spinning hook to head** while **guard stays up**. First competitive use: **Ch 98** vs Min-ho.
