### Berserker Rage (Post-Death Trigger)

**Activates when:** Hard training push **or** critical hit (head shot, knockdown, grief flash).

**Effects:** Faster/stronger kicks · speed up · **defense down** · red tint · reckless pressure.

**Growth arc:** Arc 7 loss = read the spin, lose the war on **points**. Arc 8 win = **control** over explosion.
