## Arc-by-Arc Flow Notes

### Arc 1 — New Beginnings (Ch 1–10) ✅
- **Hook:** Prime neglect → KO bounty → hook kick → wake (strong Solo Leveling dread).
- **Turn:** Kwon's window contrast [REAL #20] → MV patience → Korea U20 ask.
- **Payoff:** Team text → six arrivals → *Welcome to Kwon's.*
- **Flow check:** FLASH/PRESENT tags on Ch 1 montage prevent confusion. Hospital + threats seed Arc 4–6.

### Arc 2 — The Team (Ch 11–20) ✅
- **Structure:** One reason per member → rules → footwork → dinner → Prime flash → montage → evals → flyer.
- **Haikyuu:** Ch 12 quiz, Ch 14 ramen, Ch 17 sync reset, Ch 20 huddle.
- **Blue Lock:** Kian/Ethan mirror without betrayal; MV “fight like color belts.”
- **Bridge to Arc 3:** Tournament flyer — emotional bookend to Ch 1 tournament trauma.

### Arc 3 — First Tournament (Ch 21–35) ✅
- **Present spine:** Gear → venue → travel → Ethan loss → team fights → MV evals.
- **Flash spine (Ch 26–30):** Now **20 panels each** — board break, go fight, 20-min scold land with weight.
- **Continuity:** Ch 25 loss mirrors Ch 1; Ch 22 MV line contrasts REAL #28.
- **Pineapple Fanta:** Ch 34 thread seeds Yuna Arc 5–7.

### Arc 4 — The Shadow School (Ch 36–50) ✅
- **Function:** Prime rot catalogued — not a single villain fight.
- **Peak:** Ch 43 ultimatum, Ch 45 hospital ribbon, Ch 50 letting go on mat.
- **Tone balance:** Ch 49 comedy relief before Arc 5 grief (essential pacing).

### Arc 5 — The Road to Korea (Ch 51–70) ✅
- **Stakes rise:** Travel → camp → **grandmother death** → berserker enters kit.
- **Haikyuu:** Team in hallway; nobody alone at funeral.
- **Blue Lock:** Ethan/Kian individual paths; selection anxiety.
- **Yuna:** Ch 63–65 Pineapple Fanta — pays off Arc 7 Ch 108.

### Arc 6 — Korea Regionals (Ch 71–90) ✅
- **Scale shift:** Korea = bigger stage (Solo Leveling “dungeon tier” feel without fantasy).
- **Ch 77:** Lee Ji-woo fight — loss that teaches endurance over flash.
- **Team arc:** Placements, golden point, photo — group still wins *together* even when individuals fall.

### Arc 7 — Legends (Ch 91–110) ✅
- **Sequential read enforced:** Ch 94→100 fight order locked in outcomes.
- **Blue Lock peak:** One chapter per exit; gam-jeom, read combos, elimination on points.
- **Haikyuu peak:** Ch 100 Logan corner (*It counts*); Ch 106 nobody cuts alone.
- **Ethan arc:** Ch 97 advance → **Ch 98 corners TJ** → **Ch 99 last fight · growth not victory** → Ch 118 collegiate win + Yuna — **not broken**.

### Arc 8 — Closure (Ch 111–120) ✅
- **Emotional denouement:** No boss fight with Ttong — pity + peace.
- **Epilogue:** Ch 118 collegiate win with rage **declined** — completes berserker arc.
- **Final message:** Ch 119 character over rank · Ch 120 team + memorial photo.
