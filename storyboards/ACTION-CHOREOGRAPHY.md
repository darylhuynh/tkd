## Research Basis (What “Modern Olympic” Means)

Choreography follows patterns documented in elite competition analysis and Paris 2024 rules — not generic “martial arts.”

| Source | Use in story |
|--------|----------------|
| [NBC Olympics — Paris 2024 scoring](https://www.nbcolympics.com/news/taekwondo-101-olympic-rules-regulations-and-scoring) | Point values, best-of-3 **rounds**, tie-breakers |
| [Menescardi et al. 2019 — Olympic medalist tactics](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC6914813/) | Cut → spin, dodge → head, counter on opponent’s cut |
| [TKD Wellness — Olympic combinations](https://www.tkdwellness.com/top-taekwondo-moves-for-olympic-competition/) | Roundhouse chains, back kick surprise, axe/side for distance |
| [SportsEdTV — Kyorugi rules](https://sportsedtv.com/blog/key-rules-of-kyorugi-taekwondo-sparring) | Legal targets, PSS sensors, referee flow |

**In-world rule note:** Regional/U20 matches use **1:30 rounds** (story canon). **Olympic/collegiate finals** mirror Paris 2024: **2:00 rounds**, score **resets each round**, win **2 of 3 rounds**.

## Paris 2024 Scoring (Canonical for Fights)

| Technique | Points | PSS / judges |
|-----------|--------|--------------|
| Punch to hogu | **1** | Manual |
| Kick to trunk | **2** | Automatic |
| Kick to head | **3** | Automatic |
| **Turning** kick to trunk | **4** | PSS + 2 judges confirm turn |
| **Turning** kick to head | **5** | PSS + 2 judges confirm turn |
| Opponent gam-jeom | **+1 to you** | Referee |

*Kieryn’s line stays:* **“Head is three. Turning is double.”**

**Not allowed (ref stops / penalty):** grabbing hogu, pushing, kicks below waist, elbow/knee, turning your back to flee, excessive clinch.

## What Olympic Sparring *Looks* Like (Visual Checklist)

Use these in **every** fight prompt so art reads “WT kyorugi,” not anime street fight:

| Element | Description | AI prompt fragment |
|---------|-------------|-------------------|
| **Octagon mat** | Blue/red corners, WT logo center | `olympic taekwondo octagonal mat blue red corners` |
| **PSS gear** | **Full kyorugi kit** on both fighters — white dobok + electronic chest hogu + headgear (face shield) + forearm guards + shin/foot guards | `white dobok full olympic kyorugi gear electronic chest hogu headgear forearm guards shin foot guards` |
| **Fighting stance** | Side-on, guard high, chin tucked, bounce on balls of feet | `olympic kyorugi fighting stance side profile guard up` |
| **Distance bands** | Long (probe cut kick) → mid (roundhouse) → clinch (punch / illegal grab reset) | `long range taekwondo distance footwork` |
| **Front leg** | Fast cut kick, axe kick, push kick — **sets rhythm** | `front leg cut kick quick tap to hogu` |
| **Back leg** | Power roundhouse, back kick, spinning — **finishes** | `back leg turning roundhouse hip rotation` |
| **Footwork** | In-out, angle steps, no wild running | `taekwondo skip step in-out distance control` |
| **Corner** | Coach + 1, towel, hand signals | `taekwondo corner coach hand signals` |

**Flashy ≠ illegal:** Height comes from **speed, rotation, sensor flash, crowd blur** — not wire-fu or **4+ kicks in the air** without planting. *(Ariana's **Whirling Dervish** stays legal: **3 kicks — then foot down**.)*

## Elite Tactical Patterns (Medalist Research → Panel Beats)

From Olympic medalist analysis — use as **exchange templates**:

### Pattern A — Cut kick trap → spinning score (2–5 pts)
1. Fighter A throws **2–3 front-leg cut kicks** (quick hogu tap, no push)
2. Fighter B tries to **close distance** or answer with cut
3. Fighter A **reads the cut**, steps short, **spinning back kick** or **turning head kick**
4. **PSS flash** + judges’ turning flag → **4 or 5 points**

*Story use:* Kieryn (Arc 3 Ch 33), Ethan growth (Arc 7 **Ch 98**)

### Pattern B — Dodge → indirect head (3 pts)
1. Opponent commits to linear roundhouse
2. Fighter **slides head off-line** (dodge), stays in range
3. **Back leg head kick** or **hook kick to head** on the open side
4. Sensor flash **+3**

*Story use:* Ethan counter style, Arc 6 endurance wins

### Pattern C — Direct body → head chain (1 → 3)
1. **Punch or fast body roundhouse** (+1 or +2) — forces guard down
2. Same beat or next beat: **head kick** over dropping hands (+3)
3. Corner screams **“HEAD!”**

*Story use:* TJ Emperor’s Kick setup (body first), Arc 3 Ch 32

### Pattern D — Volume → counter (Ethan vs. rage)
1. Opponent **flurries** body kicks (Olympic “activity” scoring pressure)
2. Calm fighter **blocks/clinches once**, resets
3. **Single back kick** to trunk on exit (+2) — textbook MV drill

*Story use:* Ethan **control** vs. Berserker (Arc 7 Ch 94, Ch 118)

### Pattern E — Posterior counter on cut (PCA)
1. Opponent throws cut kick
2. Fighter **turns with** the attack, **back kick** into follow-through (+2)
3. Or **spinning hook** if close (+5 if turning head confirmed)

*Story use:* **Arc 3 Ch 25 trauma** (Ethan receives) → **Arc 7 Ch 98 redemption** (Ethan delivers Ultimate Counter)

## 12-Panel Fight Template

For **major matches**, storyboard **minimum 10–14 panels** — not 3. Copy this block into arc files when expanding.

| # | Beat | Camera | Prompt core |
|---|------|--------|-------------|
| 1 | Wide — octagon, names, score 0–0 | Establishing | `olympic taekwondo arena wide octagon` |
| 2 | Guard bounce — both side-on | Low angle | `two fighters kyorugi guard referee between` |
| 3 | **Probe** — cut kick or fake | Profile | `front leg cut kick tap hogu` |
| 4 | Answer — opponent roundhouse | Action | `roundhouse kick block guard spark` |
| 5 | Corner inset — MV hand signal | Cutaway | `coach corner hand signal TWO` |
| 6 | **Setup** — 2nd cut or feint body | Medium | `feint body kick drop guard` |
| 7 | **Main technique** — chamber visible | **Slow-mo inset [TEACH]** | `spinning back kick chamber hip rotation diagram` |
| 8 | **Impact** — PSS LED flash | Extreme | `electronic headgear sensor flash impact speed lines` |
| 9 | Scoreboard insert | Graphic | `scoreboard plus five turning head kick` |
| 10 | Reaction — crowd / corner / face | Emotional | `fighter exhale crowd roar` |
| 11 | Reset — bow or clinch break | Wide | `referee reset fighters distance` |
| 12 | Consequence — win/loss/exhaustion | Character | `fighter kneeling or fist pump` |

**SFX vocabulary:** *BEEP* (PSS), *SHHH* (foot slide), *TAK* (pad), whistle, crowd swell.
