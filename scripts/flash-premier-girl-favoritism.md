# CHAPTER 37 — THE FAVORITISM ARCHIVE

---

### SCENE 1 — PROMOTION WALL [REAL #27]
**Panels:** 37.3

**ACTION:**  
Premier dojang — **belt promotion chart** on wall — names + dates.

Ethan (13–14 in flashback) scans:

**Girls' column:** three promotions in six months — **Ha-eun**, others — stickers, stars.

**Boys' column:** Ethan — top drill scores — **no new stripe** in eight months. Another boy **passed Ha-eun on forms** — still white stripe.

**KIERYN** *(future — not there yet; or Ethan internal)*  
Girls move faster. Boys wait.

**TTONG KIM** *(to parents' night crowd)*  
Our girls program is **special**. They represent the school. Patience, boys. Belt isn't a race.

Ethan's jaw tightens. He won every scrimmage last month.

---

### SCENE 2 — MAT TIME [REAL #5, #6, #9]
**Panels:** 37.2 (existing) · 37.4

**ACTION:**  
Line drill — Ethan **first** — cleanest kick.

Ttong Kim points at **Ha-eun** — middle of pack — for **demo**.

**TTONG KIM**  
Ha-eun-ah, show them spirit. That's what sponsors like.

Ha-eun smiles — looks at instructor **too long** — then kicks.

Ethan steps back into line — invisible.

---

### SCENE 3 — NEW YEAR'S MONDAY [REAL #25] ★
**Panels:** 37.5

**ACTION:**  
First class after winter break. Kids on mat — stretching — gossip energy.

**HA-EUN** *(loud enough for the row to hear — not shouting)*  
No, it was **fine**. I slept at **Kim-saab's** house. New Year's. His wife made soup.

Silence — half a beat — then whispers.

**BOY A**  
*(whisper)*  
…What?

**GIRL B**  
*(whisper)*  
She's kidding.

**HA-EUN**  
*(shrugs, smile)*  
Ask him.

**ACTION:**  
Ttong Kim at clipboard by door — **heard everything** — looks up — **doesn't deny, doesn't confirm** — small **smile** — back to paperwork.

Ethan tying belt — hands still.

**ETHAN (V.O.)**  
I didn't know if she was bragging.  
I didn't know if I was supposed to tell anyone.  
I knew I wasn't supposed to **ask**.

He never told his parents. Never told Logan. Stored it like the board break hour.

---

### SCENE 4 — BONUS POINTS [REAL #9, #24]
**Panels:** 37.6 · cross-ref Ch 28.7

**ACTION:**  
Testing day — same as board break script — Ha-eun gets **+attitude** line item — Ethan drops to second.

**TTONG KIM**  
Extra credit for **school spirit**.

Ha-eun hugs his arm. Ethan watches with bruised hand in pocket.

# CHAPTER 42 — GIRL BULLY RETURNS [REAL #26]

---

### SCENE 5 — WELCOME BACK
**Panels:** 42.1–42.2

**ACTION:**  
Months later — or same year — Premier lobby.

**Ha-eun** — or **same inner-circle girl** — walks in with mother. Younger kid from summer camp **flinches** by water fountain.

Rumor: she **grabbed** a white belt's hogu strap until he cried — school **asked her to leave**.

Today:

**TTONG KIM**  
*(to class)*  
We **forgive** people who grow. Ha-eun-ah is family again. No comments.

**VICTIM'S MOTHER** *(earlier, off-panel O.S. in flashback)*  
She hurt my son.

**TTONG KIM** *(O.S.)*  
Boys need to **toughen up**. She's valuable to our program.

**ACTION:**  
Ethan — older now — stepped between them once. Extra **conditioning** for a week. Ttong Kim: *"Don't play hero."*

**KIAN** *(if present in flashback)*  
*(not there — seed only)*

Present-day Ethan remembers when he reads Kieryn's later line: *"That school protects money, not kids."*

---

### SCENE 6 — EARLY PROMOTION QUESTION [REAL #27 → #4]
**Panels:** 43.0 bridge

**ACTION:**  
Ethan — after Ha-eun's **early stripe** — asks in front of two parents:

**ETHAN**  
*(quiet)*  
I scored higher on the last test. Why did—

**TTONG KIM**  
*(cutting)*  
**You are either with us or against us.**

Room cold. Ethan nods. Doesn't ask again.
