# CHAPTER 107 — THE BOARD

---

### SCENE 1 — NATIONAL TRAINING CENTER · ANNEX HALL · AFTERNOON
**Panels:** 107.1

**ACTION:**  
Concrete hall. Korean and English on banners. **U20 NATIONAL TEAM — FINAL SELECTION** posted on digital board — names loading one line at a time.

Kwon's team in Kwon's hoodies — muddy, exhausted, alive.

Other schools stretch, pray, cry.

**LOGAN**  
*(reading over someone's shoulder)*  
They're doing it alphabetically. That's evil.

**KIERYN**  
*(arms crossed — no tremble)*  
They're doing it slow. That's worse.

**KIAN**  
*(jaw tight — quarterfinal exit still fresh)*  
If my name flashes I swear I'll—

**KIERRYN**  
It won't. We know the bracket.

**KIAN**  
*(beat — honest)*  
Yeah. We know.

**ARIANA**  
*(small voice — first round loss days ago)*  
I didn't know I could lose that fast and still…  
*(touch chest)*  
…still want tomorrow.

**MR. YANG**  
*(off to side — came for Ariana — arms folded — proud)*  
That's the sport, anak. Not your father's checklist.

She nods. Doesn't hide the smile this time.

**ACTION:**  
Board updates:

```
−68kg M  ·  ·  ·  LIM, TAE-JOON  ✓
```

Silence.

**TJ**  
*(staring — doesn't celebrate yet)*  
That's… me.

**LOGAN**  
**TJ!!**

**TJ**  
*(hand up — stop)*  
Wait. Wait for everyone else's truth.

**MV**  
*(proud — contained)*  
Accept yours when it comes, TJ. You earned the wait.

**[END SCENE 1]**

---

### SCENE 2 — THE LIST COMPLETES
**Panels:** 107.1 cont.

**ACTION:**  
More names scroll. Other schools erupt. Kwon's kids hold breath.

**−45kg M** category loads.

Ethan watches — still — gold headphones around neck — not on.

Names fill. Not his.

**ETHAN**  
*(internal)*  
I read the kick. I still lost the war.

Flash inset — **one panel** — **Ch 98** Ultimate Counter **+5** landing clean. Growth without trophy.

Board finalizes. Ethan Hyun — **not listed**.

**LOGAN**  
*(quiet — unusual)*  
Hyung…

**ETHAN**  
*(before anyone apologizes)*  
TJ made it.

**TJ**  
*(turns — eyes wet — guilty and happy — both)*  
Ethan—

**ETHAN**  
*(steps in — hug — tight — quick)*  
Yeah. You did.

**TJ**  
I wanted us both—

**ETHAN**  
You got national team. That's not half a dream. That's the whole flag.

**MV**  
*(to team — loud enough for hall)*  
One name on that board wears our patch. **Six names in this room** carry it. Don't forget which number matters.

**[HAIKYUU — celebrate teammate without shrinking loss]**

**[END CHAPTER 107]**

# CHAPTER 108 — NEXT YEAR & YUNA

---

### SCENE 3 — NEXT YEAR CORNER
**Panels:** 108.1

**ACTION:**  
Side corridor. Vending machines humming. Team debrief without formality.

**KIERYN**  
Semifinals.  
*(kick tile)*  
Next year.

**KIAN**  
Quarters.  
*(grin — feral but real)*  
Same. I'm younger. I'll catch your semis on the way up.

**KIERRYN**  
*(almost smile)*  
Dream big, bean pole.

**ARIANA**  
Round one. Lost by **one**. Gam-jeom on the tenth kick.  
*(beat)*  
I still love this. Dad heard me say it out loud. I think he believed me.

**LOGAN**  
I didn't fight. But I was there. That counts, right?

**TJ**  
*(ruffling Logan)*  
You screamed so much my ears are still bleeding.

**ETHAN**  
*(leaning wall — exhausted)*  
Ttong Kim said I couldn't do school and taekwondo.

**KIERYN**  
*(sharp)*  
Ttong Kim can—

**ETHAN**  
I didn't make U20. I'm still taking the SNU exam. Still training.  
*(beat)*  
Watch me do both.

Silence — respect silence.

**MV**  
Points measure a moment. Not a person. Legends aren't only on rosters.

**[END SCENE 3]**

---

### SCENE 4 — CONCOURSE · YUNA · SAME TIME
**Panels:** 108.2 + flashback inset

**ACTION:**  
Yuna Park — poomsae dobok swapped for plain clothes — stands half-hidden behind pillar near **gear check tables**.

She watched Ethan's last match from the upper concourse. She watched the board from here.

She saw the hug.

In her bag: one **Pineapple Fanta** — yellow can — cold against her palm.

**FLASHBACK INSET — ARC 3 · VENDING MACHINE · DESATURATED**

Younger Ethan. Half headgear. Can drops. Sip. Shoulders drop one inch.

**LOGAN (V.O.)**  
*Halmeoni used to buy it for him after school.*

**YUNA (V.O.)**  
*Wrong moment in Korea. Not a rescue drink.*

**BACK TO PRESENT**

She walks to Kwon's gear row. Ethan's bag slumped with everyone else's.

She sets the can on top — deliberate — label facing out.

Small sticky note — no hearts — neat handwriting:

> **Yellow only. — Y**

She's three steps away when Ethan turns the corner.

He sees the can.

Stops.

Looks around — corridor almost empty — catches **the back of her ponytail** turning at the far exit.

He doesn't chase.

Picks up the can. Cold. Right color.

**ETHAN**  
*(whisper)*  
…Pineapple.

Opens it. *Pssht.* Same half-inch shoulder drop.

Not happy. Not fixed.

**Held.**

**LOGAN**  
*(appearing)*  
Was that a gift? Was that a **girl** gift? Hyung, is that—

**ETHAN**  
*(one warning look)*  
Logan.

**LOGAN**  
*(zips lips — mimes locking)*  
I'll die with the secret. …Unless Kieryn asks. I fold in four seconds.

Ethan almost laughs. Almost.

**[END CHAPTER 108]**

# CHAPTER 109 — PARKING LOT

---

### SCENE 5 — PARKING LOT · SUNSET
**Panels:** 109.1

**ACTION:**  
Kwon's van. Orange sky. Team loads gear.

**TJ**  
*(national team lanyard already on — awkward — honored)*  
I don't know how to wear this yet.

**KIAN**  
Wear it like you belong. You do.

**KIERRYN**  
*(to Ethan)*  
SNU application — send it. Semifinals me, quarters him, round one her — we all got homework.

**ARIANA**  
*(nods)*  
Me too.

**ETHAN**  
*(Fanta can empty — crushed in recycling bin — label still yellow)*  
Yeah. Homework.

**MV**  
*(closes van door)*  
Team photo when we land. Same mat. Different chapter.

**[END CHAPTER 109]**

# CHAPTER 110 — EMBRACE ANYWAY

---

### SCENE 6 — GROUP HUG · DEPARTURE
**Panels:** 110.1

**ACTION:**  
Group hug — spontaneous — **TJ center** — Logan crying happy — Kian complaining he's squished — Kieryn denying she cares — Ariana **hugs back** — Ethan in the middle — not lost — **part of**.

Wide shot: van pulls away from national center.

Insert: digital board still glowing inside building — **LIM, TAE-JOON** — one name — enough for all of them.

**MV (V.O.)**  
One roster spot. Six futures. That's still Kwon's.
