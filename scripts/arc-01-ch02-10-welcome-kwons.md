# CHAPTER 2 — ARRIVAL

### SCENE 1 — WINDOW [REAL #20]
**Panels:** 2.1 · 2.2

**ACTION:**  
Evening. **Kwon's Taekwondo Academy** — wood floors — amber light.

Ethan **peeks through window** — doesn't enter.

Inside: **MV** adjusts one student's foot angle — eye level — patient.

Split montage — Premier flash: crowd, instructor's **back**, noise.

**ETHAN (V.O.)**  
One student. One voice.

Hand on door. Hesitates.

**[END SCENE 1]**

---

# CHAPTER 3 — MEETING MASTER VIET

### SCENE 2 — INVITED IN
**Panels:** 3.1 · 3.2 · 3.4 [CARD optional]

**ACTION:**  
MV notices Ethan at glass. Opens door — no sales pitch.

**MV**  
You looking or training?

**ETHAN**  
*(long beat)*  
…Training.

Other students glance — **silver-letter black belt** visible when he bows.

**MV**  
*(calm)*  
Show me what you kept when you left.

**[END SCENE 2]**

---

# CHAPTER 4 — DEMONSTRATION [TEACH]

### SCENE 3 — PADDLES
**Panels:** 4.1–4.4

**ACTION:**  
MV runs basics — roundhouse, back kick, cut kick — shield and paddles.

Ethan executes — **flawless** — barely speaks.

**MV**  
*(genuine)*  
Your form is better than most black belts I see.  
*(beat)*  
Why would you leave a school that had you?

**ETHAN**  
*(stares at floor)*  
They didn't have me.

**[REAL #21 — perfect form compliment]**

**[END SCENE 3]**

---

# CHAPTER 5 — THE GOAL

### SCENE 4 — WHAT DO YOU WANT?
**Panels:** 5.1 · 5.2

**ACTION:**  
MV sits on bench — Ethan stands — formal distance narrowing.

**MV**  
What do you want from taekwondo?

Silence. Ethan almost lies small.

**ETHAN**  
Korea U20 National Team.

Room still. MV doesn't laugh.

**MV**  
That's not a small dream.

**ETHAN**  
I know.

**MV**  
Good. Small dreams don't survive hard weeks.

**ETHAN**  
Can I… train here?

**MV**  
*(no promise)*  
We'll see if you mean it.

**[END SCENE 4]**

---

# CHAPTER 6 — WHY DID YOU LEAVE?

### SCENE 5 — DEFLECTION
**Panels:** 6.1 · 6.3

**ACTION:**  
MV asks direct: *Why did you leave?*

Ethan opens mouth — closes it.

**FLASH STING — woven on question** *(STYLE: PREMIER)*

Hands exchanging cash. **Ttong Kim** cold face. Red tint.

**TTONG KIM (V.O. — distorted)**  
…with us or…

**FLASH — VIP [REAL #5]** — paying kid adjusted — Ethan on bleachers in same memory frame.

**PRESENT — STYLE: KWONS**

**ETHAN**  
It wasn't home anymore.

**MV**  
*(nods — accepts for now)*  
When you're ready to tell me the rest, I'll listen.

**[END SCENE 5]**

---

# CHAPTER 7 — FLASHBACKS BEGIN

### SCENE 6 — WARM THEN COLD [REAL #5 · #6]
**Panels:** 7.1 · 7.2

**ACTION:**  
Flashback — small empty Premier. Young Ethan white belt bows.

**TTONG KIM (younger, warmer)**  
Welcome to the family.

Montage — enrollment grows — VIP brochures — instructor's smile **thins** — Ethan wins drills — sidelined for demo picks.

**ETHAN (V.O.)**  
He was kind when the room was empty.

**[END SCENE 6]**

---

# CHAPTER 8 — HOSPITAL [REAL #14 · #15]

### SCENE 7 — VISIT
**Panels:** 8.1 · 8.2 · 8.3

**ACTION:**  
After training. Hospital room — **Halmeoni** frail — kind tired eyes.

Ethan holds her hand. Phone buzzes — spam — then **voicemail**:

**TTONG KIM (V.O.)**  
*(filtered, threatening)*  
Stop talking about my school. Stop making kids leave.

Ethan silences phone. Sits closer.

**HALMEONI**  
*(weak Korean)*  
Bad men on the phone?

**ETHAN**  
*(Korean)*  
Nothing. Rest.

**HALMEONI**  
You came. That's enough for today.

**[END SCENE 7]**

---

# CHAPTER 9 — CAN THEY COME TOO?

### SCENE 8 — EVERYONE DESERVES A HOME
**Panels:** 9.1 · 9.2

**ACTION:**  
Kwon's — Ethan finds MV rolling mats alone.

**ETHAN**  
Some friends… they left Premier too. Can they join?

**MV**  
Everyone deserves a home.

Ethan texts — screen shows names — **TJ · Kieryn · Logan · Kian · Ariana** — no faces yet.

**ETHAN (V.O.)**  
I didn't ask them to follow me. I asked if they could breathe.

**[END SCENE 8]**

---

# CHAPTER 10 — WELCOME TO KWON'S

### SCENE 9 — SIX SILHOUETTES
**Panels:** 10.1–10.10

**ACTION:**  
Next session. Door opens — **five arrivals** — varied body language:

- **TJ** — confident stride · teal headband energy  
- **Kieryn** — sarcastic lean · purple-teal bob  
- **Logan** — cheerful wave · bowl cut · too much gear  
- **Kian** — silver hair · hands in pockets · challenging smirk  
- **Ariana** — red streak bob · silent · extra bandages  

MV gathers them — Ethan off to side — not hiding — not center.

**MV**  
Same rules for everyone. We train hard. We don't tear each other down.  
*(looks at Ethan)*  
You brought family. Don't waste it.

Team glances at Ethan — not pity — **curiosity**.

**TJ**  
*(to Ethan)*  
You picked a place with windows. Good call.

**KIERYN**  
If the mats are soft, I'm still leaving.

**LOGAN**  
Is there food later?

**KIAN**  
*(to Ethan)*  
You owe me a rematch from Premier.

**ARIANA**  
*(stretching — doesn't look up)*  
…

Wide shot — full team on mat — MV center — amber light.

**CAPTION:** *Welcome to Kwon's.*

**[END CHAPTER 10 · END ARC 1]**
