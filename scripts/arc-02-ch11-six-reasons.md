# CHAPTER 11 — SIX REASONS

---

### SCENE 1 — CIRCLE ON THE MAT
**Panels:** 11.1

**ACTION:**  
After class. Mats rolled half. Team sits circle — MV standing — empty chair beside Ethan.

**MV**  
You don't owe me your past. But your teammates might need to hear it.

Beat. Nobody rushes.

**KIAN**  
*(stands)*  
I'll go. Someone has to.

**[END SCENE 1]**

---

### SCENE 2 — KIAN [REAL — humiliation]
**Panels:** 11.2

**FLASH — PRIME**

**Ttong Kim** center mat — **entire class** in lines — **Kian** singled out — face red.

**TTONG KIM**  
You think this is a **joke**? You act like a **clown** — you train like trash.

Laughter from favored row. Kian's fists shake.

**PRESENT**

**KIAN**  
I wasn't laughing after that. I was done performing for him.

**KIERRYN**  
*(quiet)*  
Good.

**[END SCENE 2]**

---

### SCENE 3 — KIERYN
**Panels:** 11.3

**FLASH — PRIME**

Friends on mat — wave — **Kieryn** at door — bag packed.

**KIERYN (PRESENT)**  
My friends were there. But I'd never get better. Not at Prime.  
*(beat)*  
I wasn't abandoning them. I was refusing to rot.

**TJ**  
They're here now. That counts.

**[END SCENE 3]**

---

### SCENE 4 — ARIANA
**Panels:** 11.4

**FLASH — PRIME**

**Mr. Yang** handshake with **Ttong Kim** — **Ariana** in corner — ignored — re-wrapping same wrist.

**FATHER (O.S.)**  
She needs a coach who **sees** her.

**ARIANA (PRESENT)**  
He listened when they didn't.

**LOGAN**  
*(soft)*  
Mr. Yang's scary. In a good way.

**[END SCENE 4]**

---

### SCENE 5 — LOGAN
**Panels:** 11.5

**FLASH — PRIME**

**Logan** grabs **Ethan's** arm at door — gear bag too big — both walking out.

**LOGAN (PRESENT)**  
Where you go, I go. I don't need to compete. I need my brother.

Ethan's throat tight — doesn't look away.

**[END SCENE 5]**

---

### SCENE 6 — TJ
**Panels:** 11.6

**FLASH — PRIME**

**Ttong Kim** points at **TJ** alone — other students watch — TJ's form **sloppier** than memory of old school.

**TJ (PRESENT)**  
He singled me out every day. I was getting **worse**. Ethan was at Kwon's. I followed my friend.

**KIAN**  
*(grin — no bite)*  
Anchor follows anchor.

**[END SCENE 6]**

---

### SCENE 7 — ETHAN LAST [HAIKYUU]
**Panels:** 11.7

**ACTION:**  
Circle quiet. All eyes on Ethan. He could give the catalogue — board break, favoritism, head kick, twenty-minute scold — he doesn't.

One line.

**ETHAN**  
I was good enough to be ignored.

Silence — not awkward — **Karasuno hearing Hinata** energy.

**TJ**  
*(firm)*  
**Not here.**

**KIERYN**  
Not here.

**LOGAN**  
*(nods hard)*  
Not here.

**KIAN** · **ARIANA**  
*(same)*  
Not here.

**MV**  
*(small smile)*  
Good. Now we train like we mean it.

**[HAIKYUU — team circle]**

**[END CHAPTER 11]**
