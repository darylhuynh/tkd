# CHAPTER 111 — AFTER SELECTION

---

### SCENE 1 — KWON'S DOJANG · EARLY MORNING
**Panels:** 111.1

**ACTION:**  
Week after the van came home. Orange light through Kwon's windows — same amber as Arc 1 Ch 2.

Mats being rolled out. MV in back office — coffee, paperwork.

**Ethan** alone at the **entrance** — hand on door frame, not the handle.

Same hesitation. Different body.

**ETHAN (V.O.)**  
Arc 1 — I stood here because I didn't trust anyone.  
Arc 7 — I stood in a hallway in Korea because TJ's name was on a board and mine wasn't.  
Same feeling. Different reason.

**ACTION:**  
He steps in. No headphones. Silver-letter belt tied — deliberate.

Empty mat. His spot — third line from mirror — where MV first watched him drill.

He bows to the room. No one else there.

**ETHAN (V.O.)**  
TJ made it. I didn't.  
*(footwork — slow)*  
Not the end.

---

### SCENE 2 — FIRST CLASS BACK
**Panels:** 111.1 cont.

**ACTION:**  
Team trickles in. **TJ** — national-team warm-up jacket — tries to take it off before anyone sees. Too late.

**LOGAN**  
*(whisper-shout)*  
He's wearing the jacket. He's wearing THE jacket.

**TJ**  
It's a jacket, not a crown.

**KIAN**  
It's both. Take the both.

**KIERRYN**  
If you bow slower because of that lanyard I will kick you.

**TJ**  
*(small smile)*  
Please don't. National team insurance is confusing.

**ACTION:**  
Line forms. MV counts. Normal drill — front switch, cut kick, reset.

Ethan in line — **not** beside TJ by rank — beside **Ariana**. Workhorse pace. No showing off.

**MV**  
*(during water break)*  
Ethan. Office. Two minutes.

Team glances. Not worried — MV pulls everyone aside sometimes.

---

### SCENE 3 — MV OFFICE
**Panels:** 111.1 cont.

**ACTION:**  
Small room. Trophies from regional years. Photo of MV's own coach, faded.

MV doesn't sit behind desk like a principal. Stands — equal height.

**MV**  
You came back first today.

**ETHAN**  
Couldn't sleep.

**MV**  
Neither could half the team. You weren't alone in that.

**ETHAN**  
I know.

**MV**  
Do you?

**ETHAN**  
*(beat)*  
TJ got the roster. I got… the same mat I started on.

**MV**  
And?

**ETHAN**  
And I don't know if that's enough.

**MV**  
*(not soft — honest)*  
It's not supposed to be enough forever. It's supposed to be **true** today.

He slides an envelope across desk — **서울대학교 — Seoul National University** — application receipt / **early decision pending** stamp.

**MV**  
You submitted this before Korea. You forgot?

**ETHAN**  
I forgot everything except the bracket.

**MV**  
Grades don't care about brackets. Neither does Seoul.  
*(beat)*  
One roster spot. Six futures. I meant that in the parking lot.

**ETHAN**  
*(touching envelope)*  
I thought SNU was… if I failed at taekwondo, at least I—

**MV**  
Stop.  
*(firm)*  
You want school **and** taekwondo. That's not a backup plan. That's a whole person.

Ethan flinches — Arc 4 parent gossip ghost — doesn't explain why.

**MV**  
Train this morning like you're still chasing something. Because you are.

**ETHAN**  
Yes, sir.

**MV**  
And Ethan — welcome back to the mat.

**ACTION:**  
Ethan exits — envelope in gear bag — almost smiles — doesn't yet.

**[END CHAPTER 111]**

# CHAPTER 112 — FORMER CLASSMATES

---

### SCENE 1 — KWON'S LOBBY · SATURDAY AFTERNOON
**Panels:** 112.1

**ACTION:**  
Parents in parking lot. Kids in street clothes at Kwon's glass door — **Prime logo hoodies** — peering in like the building might bite.

**Min-jun** (16, Prime) — recognizes Ethan through glass — raises hand half-wave.

**So-yeon** (15) — beside him — arms wrapped tight — white belt bag, old Prime patch half-peeled.

Ethan opens door. Team training audible in back — Kieryn yelling at Kian about something stupid.

**MIN-JUN**  
Hey. We… uh. We heard TJ made national team.

**ETHAN**  
Yeah.

**MIN-JUN**  
That's cool. That's really cool.

**SO-YEON**  
*(to floor)*  
Kim-saab said he always knew TJ was special.

**KIAN**  
*(from mat — not hiding)*  
Special like paid VIP special or special like actually good special?

**MV**  
*(appearing)*  
Kian.

**KIAN**  
*(mutters)*  
…Special special.

---

### SCENE 2 — HONEST, NOT RECRUITING
**Panels:** 112.1

**ACTION:**  
Lobby bench. Ethan sits — doesn't perform Kwon's sales pitch.

**MIN-JUN**  
People at Prime ask about you. Instructor says you left because you couldn't handle pressure.

**ETHAN**  
*(no heat)*  
I left because I couldn't handle **him**.

**SO-YEON**  
*(looks up)*  
My mom says Kwon's is for quitters who need hand-holding.

Beat.

**ETHAN**  
Kwon's isn't perfect.  
*(gestures at back — team noise)*  
We fight each other. We lose. Kieryn calls us idiots. Ariana trains through dinner.  
But they **see** you. When you're good. When you're bad. When you're just… there.

**MIN-JUN**  
Kim-saab sees us when parents are watching.

**ETHAN**  
That's not the same.

**SO-YEON**  
*(quiet)*  
I don't want to go to another tournament and sit eight hours and hear **go fight**.

Ethan's jaw tightens — memory — releases.

**ETHAN**  
I'm not telling you to leave. I'm not recruiting. Prime isn't my business anymore.  
*(beat)*  
But if you're asking because you're scared — scared is honest. You don't have to perform brave for him.

**MIN-JUN**  
Why did you let us in?

**ETHAN**  
Because someone should have told me that earlier.

---

### SCENE 3 — LOGAN OFFERS WATER [HAIKYUU]
**Panels:** 112.2

**ACTION:**  
**Logan** bounds out with water bottles — Gatorade, room temp, wrong flavor — classic.

**LOGAN**  
Visitors! Hi! We're not a cult. We have mats. And water. Mostly water.

He hands **Min-jun** a bottle. Hands **So-yeon** one without asking if she wants it.

**SO-YEON**  
*(almost laugh)*  
…Thanks.

**LOGAN**  
*(to Ethan, stage whisper)*  
Are they defecting? Should I get MV? Should I hide the good paddles?

**ETHAN**  
They're asking questions. That's all.

**LOGAN**  
*(to So-yeon)*  
Questions are free. Ramen costs extra. Hyung will pay.

**ETHAN**  
I will not.

**ACTION:**  
Prime kids leave — lighter — not converted — **less alone**.

Min-jun pauses at door:

**MIN-JUN**  
Tell TJ… tell him we're not all jerks over there.

**ETHAN**  
I know.

Door closes.

**LOGAN**  
*(sip)*  
That was sad. I liked them.

**ETHAN**  
Me too.

**[END CHAPTER 112]**

# CHAPTER 113 — LAST IMAGE

---

### SCENE 1 — STREET · PRIME DOJANG BLOCK · LATE AFTERNOON
**Panels:** 113.1

**ACTION:**  
Ethan walks home the long way — always has since Arc 4.

Prime Taekwondo Sports Dynasty — strip-mall facade — **smaller parking lot than memory** — two cars.

Window into dojang: **six kids** on mat. Used to be thirty.

**TTONG KIM** — broad shoulders, expensive watch — demoing a kick — smile for parent with clipboard — back to kid who missed the stance.

**ETHAN (V.O.)**  
Arc 4 — I laughed until I cried. Third dan. He was second. **I outrank him.**  
It explained the gear obsession. The VIP lists. The **master** title on a window that never earned it.

**ACTION:**  
Ethan's gear bag — **SNU envelope** visible — corner bent from gym — **ACCEPTED** stamp through window (letter arrived this morning — he read it on the bus — hands still shaking).

He doesn't stop walking.

---

### SCENE 2 — THROUGH THE GLASS
**Panels:** 113.1 cont.

**ACTION:**  
Inside — **Ttong Kim** barks:

**TTONG KIM**  
Again. Parents paid for excellence, not excuses.

Kid resets — scared — tries.

Parent nods — checkbook energy.

**ETHAN (V.O.)**  
I feel bad for that kid.  
*(beat)*  
I used to feel bad for me the same way.

**ACTION:**  
Ttong Kim turns — catches movement in window — Ethan's silhouette passing.

Their eyes meet — one second — glass between.

Ttong Kim's smile dies. Something like **recognition** — not guilt — **calculation**.

Ethan doesn't flip him off. Doesn't bow. Keeps walking.

**ETHAN (V.O.)**  
Third dan. SNU acceptance in my bag. Collegiate league registration on my desk at home.  
Not angry.  
Pity isn't forgiveness. It's just… done.

**ACTION:**  
Wide — Ethan smaller in frame — Prime sign behind — **PRIME TAEKWONDO SPORTS DYNASTY** — word **DYNASTY** half-flicker in neon.

He rounds corner. Doesn't look back.

**[END CHAPTER 113]**

# CHAPTER 114 — THREATS END

---

### SCENE 1 — ETHAN'S ROOM · NIGHT
**Panels:** 114.1

**ACTION:**  
Desk — textbooks — SNU welcome packet printout — taekwondo collegiate registration — grandmother's photo in cheap frame.

Phone buzzes. Unknown number. Again. Again.

**INSERT — message thread (old + new):**

```
TTONG KIM (blocked 6 mo ago)
"You skipped tournaments. I don't know how committed you are."

NEW — today:
"Congrats to your friend. National team kid trained 
at MY school first. Don't forget who built you."

"Keep talking and I'll make sure every coach 
in the circuit hears what kind of punk you are."
```

**REAL #15 callback** — same voice from Arc 4 Ch 46 — make life worse if he keeps talking.

Ethan reads. Face flat. Not shaking anymore.

**LOGAN (O.S.)**  
*(door crack)*  
Hyung? You okay? Your phone's having a seizure again.

**ETHAN**  
*(without turning)*  
Old garbage.

**LOGAN**  
*(steps in — sees screen — jaw tight — rare anger)*  
He can't still—

**ETHAN**  
He can. Until I stop letting him.

---

### SCENE 2 — KWON'S · NEXT EVENING
**Panels:** 114.1 cont.

**ACTION:**  
Ethan shows MV the thread — phone screen — no drama.

MV reads. Long silence.

**MV**  
What do you want to do?

**ETHAN**  
Delete. Block. Archive for my parents if he escalates.  
*(beat)*  
I'm not twelve. I'm not scared of his rank. I'm not scared of his parents' checks.

**MV**  
Good.

**ETHAN**  
I wanted to tell you in case he contacts the dojang.

**MV**  
Let me. I'll handle the adult noise. You handle your feet.

Ethan's thumb — **Delete conversation** — **Block contact** — **Report spam**.

Each tap — clean — no speech.

**MV**  
You owe yourself peace.

**ETHAN**  
*(first time the line lands)*  
Yeah. I do.

**ACTION:**  
Ethan pockets phone. Picks up gloves. Goes to mat.

**LOGAN**  
*(from doorway — watched whole thing)*  
Can I block him on my phone too? For fun?

**MV**  
Logan.

**LOGAN**  
For justice.

**ETHAN**  
*(almost smile)*  
Get pads. We're sparring.

**[END CHAPTER 114]**

# CHAPTER 115 — GRANDMOTHER MEMORIAL

---

### SCENE 1 — HYUN FAMILY CAR · CEMETERY GATE · MORNING
**Panels:** 115.1

**ACTION:**  
Overcast. Quiet road. **Kwon's van** behind — full team — MV driving second car so family car isn't crowded.

Ethan's mother upfront — silent. Logan beside Ethan — leg bouncing — then still.

**LOGAN**  
*(whisper)*  
You don't have to say anything up there. We'll just… be car people.

**ETHAN**  
Car people?

**LOGAN**  
Like hospital people. But outside.

Ethan exhales — grateful — doesn't say it.

---

### SCENE 2 — RESTING PLACE
**Panels:** 115.1 cont.

**ACTION:**  
Simple stone — Korean and English — **HYUN, SOON-JA** — dates. Fresh flowers from mother already placed.

Ethan kneels — dobok under jacket — not performing — **Kwon's patch** pinned inside lapel — same one from Arc 5 death scene.

**ETHAN**  
Halmeoni.  
*(English — then Korean fragments)*  
I lost U20. TJ made the team. I didn't.

**ACTION:**  
Wind. Flag on veteran marker nearby — unrelated — snaps.

**ETHAN**  
I got into SNU. Early decision. Engineering… maybe. I'm still figuring that out.  
*(touch stone)*  
You wanted me good at school **and** taekwondo. I'm trying to be both. Not half of each.

**ETHAN (V.O.)**  
Arc 5 — you said *champion* like it was my name.  
Arc 7 — I lost on points. Read the kick. Still lost.  
Today — I'm not asking you to fix it.

**ETHAN**  
I still hear you say *my champion*.  
*(beat)*  
I'm still your champion. Just… not the one on that roster.

---

### SCENE 3 — HALLWAY CALLBACK [HAIKYUU]
**Panels:** 115.1 cont.

**ACTION:**  
Ethan stands. Walks back toward parking — path passes **memorial chapel hallway** — fluorescent — benches — same layout echo as **Arc 5 Ch 59 hospital hallway**.

**FLASH INSET — Arc 5:** Team slumped on benches — Logan on TJ — Kieryn mascara — Kian on floor — Ariana + Mr. Yang — MV with coffee.

**CUT TO — Present:** Same cluster — **different building** — same posture — waiting **for him**, not **for news**.

**TJ**  
*(stands)*  
Take your time.

**KIERRYN**  
No one's timing you.

**KIAN**  
*(not performing)*  
Same hallway energy. Better reason.

**ARIANA**  
*(holds printed team photo — Kwon's group shot from regional*)  
We brought this. If you want to leave it.

Ethan takes photo — edges soft — golden hour from last month's training camp.

**ETHAN**  
Yeah.

---

### SCENE 4 — TEAM PHOTO AT STONE
**Panels:** 115.2

**ACTION:**  
Ethan places photo against stone base — weighted with smooth river rock from Logan's pocket — absurd — perfect.

**LOGAN**  
Halmeoni said ramen isn't dinner. So we ate anyway after. Twice.

Team almost laughs — catches it — respectful ripple.

**MR. YANG**  
*(not here physically — text Ariana reads aloud)*  
*"God keep her. You keep training. She would want both."*  
Same words Arc 5. Ariana saved them.

**MV**  
*(at end of hallway — gives space — present)*  
When you're ready, car people become lunch people.

**ETHAN**  
*(nods)*  
Five minutes.

**ACTION:**  
Team retreats to chapel entrance — doesn't leave — **Haikyuu hallway rule** — visible through glass doors.

Ethan alone at stone one last minute.

**ETHAN (V.O.)**  
Prime had belts. Kwon's had people.  
Halmeoni had both — recipes and rules — before any coach.

**ACTION:**  
Wind lifts corner of team photo — settles — faces smiling — TJ not in national jacket — just team.

Ethan bows — deep — held — to stone.

Walks toward hallway light where six silhouettes wait.

**FADE OUT.**

**Caption (optional):** *Three months to Seoul.*

**[END CHAPTER 115]**
