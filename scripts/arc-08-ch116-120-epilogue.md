# CHAPTER 116 — FOR WHEN YOU'RE READY

---

### SCENE 1 — KWON'S DOJANG · PARKING LOT BENCH · LATE SUMMER DUSK
**Panels:** 116.1

**ACTION:**  
Heat still in the pavement. Cicadas. Ethan sits on the bench where he used to put headphones on before walking in — Arc 1 habit.

Headphones are **in his bag**, not on his ears.

**Yuna** walks up from the poomsae side exit — dobok bag over shoulder, hair in ponytail, calm as always.

She doesn't ask about the selection board. She doesn't ask about Korea.

She sits — one bench width of space between them. Not cold. Respectful.

**YUNA**  
You still come here even though you're not…  
*(gestures at nothing — "not a kid anymore")*  
…required.

**ETHAN**  
Neither do you.

**YUNA**  
Poomsae doesn't end at eighteen.  
*(beat)*  
Neither does missing people.

He looks at her. She doesn't flinch.

**ETHAN**  
Halmeoni used to sit on a bench outside my old school.  
I never told you that.

**YUNA**  
Logan did.  
*(small pause)*  
He also told me about the yellow can.

Ethan's jaw tightens — not anger. Embarrassment.

**YUNA**  
I didn't bring one today.

**ETHAN**  
I know.

**YUNA**  
I brought something else.

---

### SCENE 2 — THE RING
**Panels:** 116.1–116.2

**ACTION:**  
Small box — not jewelry-store dramatic. Simple band inside — brushed silver, no stone.

**YUNA**  
It's a **friendship ring**. Not…  
*(she almost laughs at herself)*  
…not whatever you're thinking. I'm not proposing. We're seventeen.

**ETHAN**  
*(dry)*  
Good. I can't afford ramen twice this week.

**YUNA**  
*(almost smile)*  
For when you're ready. Seoul. SNU. Whatever comes after U20.  
I'll cheer from the stands. Not loud. You know how I am.

**ETHAN**  
I know.

**ACTION:**  
He takes the ring. Turns it in his palm.

**ETHAN (V.O.)**  
Grandmother used to say *champion* like it was a job title.  
TJ got the roster. I got the letter.  
Different wins. Same mat.

**ACTION:**  
He slides the ring on — **right hand**, middle finger. Simple. Visible.

**YUNA**  
*(stands)*  
Wear it when you fight. Or don't. But I'm keeping mine.

**ACTION:**  
She shows her hand — **matching band**.

Ethan's breath catches. He doesn't grab her hand. Doesn't kiss her. Just nods.

**ETHAN**  
I'll cheer for you too. Poomsae finals. Whenever.

**YUNA**  
*(walking away — then over shoulder)*  
Yellow only. Still.

She's gone before he can answer.

He puts a hand on the ring. Almost smiles.

**HEADPHONES** stay in the bag.

# CHAPTER 117 — SCHOOL AND TAEKWONDO

---

**ACTION:**  
Split-grid montage — six paths, one family.

Kids in white belts stumble through stances. **TJ** kneels to fix a foot angle — national-team tracksuit, patient.

**TJ**  
Hips square. Emperor's Kick starts in the floor, not the air.

**KID**  
Are you famous?

**TJ**  
I'm tired. Same thing.

---

**BEAT B — KIERYN · REGIONAL CAMP · NOON**

**Kieryn** spars — clean, sharp. Coach whistles stop.

Semifinals wasn't a ceiling. Preview.

**KIERYN**  
*(toweling off)*  
Tell my brother I said preview. He'll hate that.

---

**BEAT C — KIAN · EMPTY MAT · NIGHT**

**Kian** alone — whirlwind drill, again, again. Phone on floor — quarterfinal bracket saved as wallpaper.

**KIAN (V.O.)**  
Quarters won't happen twice.  
*(whirlwind)*  
Clown's gone. Storm stays.

---

**BEAT D — LOGAN · KWON'S LOBBY**

**Logan** greets a nervous new kid — Prime transfer, maybe.

**LOGAN**  
First day? Cool. We have water. We have MV. We have Kieryn yelling. You'll love it.

---

**BEAT E — ARIANA · DOJANG · MR. YANG**

**Ariana** drills accuracy — tape marks on hogu, every kick lands.

**MR. YANG**  
*(from doorway — not coaching — just watching)*  
You don't have to win for me anymore, anak.

**ARIANA**  
*(doesn't stop kicking)*  
I know. That's why I'm still here.

**ACTION:**  
He hugs her — quick, stiff, real. She lets him.

---

**BEAT F — ETHAN · SNU CAMPUS · SEOUL · AUTUMN**

**Ethan** — campus map, Korean and English, **서울대학교 — Seoul National University** on a building sign.

Envelope in hand — **ADMISSION — COLLEGE OF ENGINEERING** (or Liberal Studies — keep flexible).

He doesn't pose. He reads the name twice like he doesn't trust ink.

### SCENE 2 — THE LINE THAT FOLLOWS YOU ★
**Panels:** 117.2

**ACTION:**  
**FLASHBACK INSET — Arc 4 · Prime · hallway glass**

Parent voice muffled through door — Ethan younger, headphones **on**, pretending not to listen.

**PARENT (O.S.)**  
I feel bad for Ethan. You want him to be good at **school AND Taekwondo**?

**TTONG KIM (O.S.)**  
*(dismissive laugh)*  
He can't even focus on one thing. The boy lives in his head.

Young Ethan — hands tighten on headphone band. Doesn't enter the room.

---

**CUT TO — PRESENT · SNU QUAD**

Ethan sits on steps — admission letter on one knee, phone on the other.

Text from **Logan:** *did u open it*

Text from **TJ:** *proud hyung. don't get soft.*

Text from **MV:** *Character first. Grades second. Kicks third.*

Ethan types back to MV: *kicks third?*

**MV:** *you know what I mean.*

**ACTION:**  
Ethan stands. Folds letter into bag next to **collegiate taekwondo registration** pamphlet — **Korea University Taekwondo League**.

**ETHAN (V.O.)**  
Third dan. SNU. Collegiate roster.  
Not angry.  
Just… done proving it to him.

**ACTION:**  
Wide shot — campus, autumn leaves, Seoul sky. He walks toward gym bus stop — dobok bag on back, **friendship ring** catching light.

### SCENE 3 — GUARD DRILL [TEACH]
**Panels:** 117.3

**ACTION:**  
Kwon's — flash-forward insert or parallel: **Ethan** (home on break) teaches a kid guard.

**ETHAN**  
Hands up. Chin down. One point is still a point — Olympic rules don't care about your feelings.

**KID**  
Did you go to the Olympics?

**ETHAN**  
I went to Seoul. Close enough for today.

**MV**

Elbows in. He said that part.

**ETHAN**  
Elbows in. Always.

# CHAPTER 118 — COLLEGIATE FINAL

---

### SCENE 1 — ARENA · COLLEGIATE CHAMPIONSHIP · SEOUL
**Panels:** 118.1 · [TEACH]

**ACTION:**  
College arena — smaller than U20 national center, louder in a different way. Banners: **KOREA UNIVERSITY TAEKWONDO LEAGUE — FINALS**.

Electronic gear — hogu, headgear, arm/leg guards. **Scoreboard** visible for reader education.

**[TEACH — corner inset before fight]**

| Rule | Reminder |
|------|----------|
| Best of 3 rounds | 1:30 each |
| Body kick | 2 pts |
| Head kick | 3 pts |
| Turning kick | ×2 multiplier |
| Punch | 1 pt |

**LOGAN**  
*(Ethan's corner — not competing — towel, water)*  
Same rules. Different crowd. You don't need rage for this one.

**ETHAN**  
*(tapping friendship ring with thumb)*  
I know.

---

### SCENE 2 — THE FIGHT
**Panels:** 118.1 (multi-page)

**ACTION:**  
Ethan vs. **collegiate opponent** — older, taller, disciplined. Not a villain — just the next wall.

**Round 1:**  
Opponent probes — body kick, 2 pts. Ethan **guard up** — no berserker drop.

**ETHAN (V.O.)**  
Rage wins seconds. Discipline wins seasons.

**ACTION:**  
Ethan reads — counter backleg roundhouse — **turning head kick** — electronic **3 → 6** with multiplier.

Crowd reacts.

**Round 2:**  
Opponent adjusts — feints high, scores body. Ethan takes hit — **flash** of red at edge of vision — grandmother's voice memory — *my champion* —

He **breathes**. Does not drop guard.

**Outside-in crescent** — sets up — second counter — **2 pts body**.

Scoreboard: **Ethan 8 — Opponent 6** entering Round 3.

---

### SCENE 3 — YUNA IN THE STANDS
**Panels:** 118.1 inset

**ACTION:**  
Upper section — **Yuna** in casual clothes, not dobok. **Friendship ring** on her hand — both hands on railing.

She doesn't scream. She watches like poomsae — total focus.

**YUNA (V.O.)**  
Arc 3 — vending machine — yellow can — he looked like procedure.  
Arc 7 — I left the can. He didn't chase me.  
Now — he's not hiding behind headphones.  
I don't need loud.

**ACTION:**  
Round 3 — exchange — Ethan ahead on points — opponent desperate — **head shot** lands on Ethan — critical hit —

**VISUAL:** Red tint at periphery — rage **offers** itself —

**ETHAN (V.O.)**  
Not today.

**ACTION:**  
He clinches. Breaks clean. **Neverending endurance** — pace, not explosion — opponent fades.

Final bell.

**SCOREBOARD:**  
**ETHAN HYUN — WINNER**

**ACTION:**  
Yuna exhales — one hand on ring — small smile.

Ethan looks up — finds her in the crowd — not a kiss blown — just a nod.

She nods back.

### SCENE 4 — PODIUM · MINIMAL
**Panels:** 117.2 callback / 118.1 end

**ACTION:**  
Gold medal — collegiate, not Olympic. Ethan accepts it — thinks of **TJ's national-team jacket** — no jealousy.

**ETHAN (V.O.)**  
Legends aren't only on rosters.  
MV said that. I finally believe it.

**ACTION:**  
Phone buzzes — group chat **Kwon's Six:**

**LOGAN:** *RAMEN. NOW.*

**KIERYN:** *He won. Don't make it weird.*

**KIAN:** *We know. Ramen anyway.*

**ARIANA:** *I'm training tomorrow. But I'll eat first.*

Ethan laughs — first full laugh in months.

Stop fighting.

**ETHAN**  
I wasn't.

**KIAN**  
I was.

**LOGAN**  
Can we get ramen?

**KIERYN**  
You're all idiots.

**ARIANA**

We're still training tomorrow.

**LOGAN**  
*(grinning)*

**KIERYN**  
Don't say arc. We're not anime.

**ETHAN**  
*(touching friendship ring under table)*  
Little late for that.

# CHAPTER 119 — LAST LESSON

---

### SCENE 1 — KWON'S · EVENING · FULL TEAM
**Panels:** 119.1–119.2

**ACTION:**  
Six in line. MV center. Sunset through dojang windows — **golden hour** starting.

No sparring. No drills. Just bow.

**MV**  
You came here broken. Some of you still are — that's honest.

**KIAN**  
Coach—

**MV**  
Let me finish.  
Prime sold **rank**. I sell **character**. A true martial artist is defined by **character**, not rank.

**ACTION:**  
Behind MV — electronic **scoreboard** on wall (practice unit) — numbers flicker — then fade in Ethan's visual memory to plain **wood grain** of the dojang wall.

**MV**  
Ethan — SNU. Collegiate champion. Third dan.  
*(beat)*  
What matters is what you do when no one is scoring.

**ETHAN**  
*(quiet)*  
Hands up. Elbows in.

**MV**  
*(almost smile)*  
Good.

**ACTION:**  
Full team bow — synchronized — held.

**TJ**  
*(after rise — to Ethan)*  
National team is still mine. You can have college.

**ETHAN**  
Deal.

They bump fists — anchor and prodigy — no hierarchy, just history.
