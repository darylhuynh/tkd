# CHAPTER 58 — THE CALL

---

### SCENE 1 — KWON'S DOJANG · LATE AFTERNOON
**Panels:** 58.1

**ACTION:**  
Regional prep camp on the mats. Six lines of footwork — sync drill. MV counts in Korean. Sweat. Autumn light through windows.

Ethan is third from the left — sharp, present. Best he's looked all arc.

**MV**  
*(counts)*  
Dul… set… front switch —

**ACTION:**  
Ethan's phone buzzes in his gear bag by the wall. Once. Twice. Again — insistent.

He flinches mid-step. Lines stutter.

**KIAN**  
*(under breath)*  
Dude. Your pocket's having a seizure.

**ACTION:**  
Ethan walks off the line. Doesn't apologize. Reads the screen.

Insert: **UNIVERSITY MEDICAL CENTER — Halmeoni Hyun**

His thumb hovers. Answers.

**ETHAN (V.O.)**  
*(phone, barely audible)*  
Hello?

Beat. His face drains.

**MV**  
*(soft, already knows)*  
Go.

Ethan grabs his bag. Headphones stay on the bench — first time we notice he leaves them.

**LOGAN**  
*(starts to follow)*  
Eth—

**TJ**  
*(hand on Logan's shoulder — firm, not unkind)*  
Let him breathe. We go when he needs us.

**MV**  
*(to Ethan at door)*  
Text when you get there.

Ethan nods once. Gone.

**[END SCENE 1]**

---

### SCENE 2 — HOSPITAL CORRIDOR · EVENING
**Panels:** 58.2

**ACTION:**  
Fluorescent hum. Linoleum. Ethan sits outside Room 412 — double doors half-closed. Hands between knees. Staring at nothing.

Time pass — montage beats (same bench, different light):

- Sun through waiting-room window → gone  
- Vending machine glow on his face  
- Nurse walks past twice  

Footsteps. Not one pair — six.

**LOGAN**  
*(small voice)*  
We took the bus.

**KIERYN**  
*(no sass — flat)*  
You didn't ask. Too bad.

**TJ**  
*(sits beside Ethan — shoulder almost touching)*  
How is she?

**ETHAN**  
*(long beat)*  
Tired.

**KIAN**  
*(standing, arms crossed — uncharacteristically still)*  
My mom used to say hospitals smell like boiled rice and fear.  
*(beat)*  
She's Korean. Dad's the Indian one. I got both — neither helps in there.

Ethan doesn't look up. Almost a twitch — not a smile.

**ARIANA**  
*(arrives with MR. YANG — Filipino, mid-40s, tired eyes, kind)*  

**MR. YANG**  
*(accent — Tagalog-English)*  
Ariana told me. We don't wait outside like strangers, anak. Not for family.

He sets a **tupperware** on the bench. Steam when lid cracks.

**MR. YANG**  
Caldereta. She eats little. But she'll smell home.

**ETHAN**  
*(voice rough)*  
She's Korean.

**MR. YANG**  
Food doesn't need a passport, Ethan.

**ACTION:**  
Ariana sits on Ethan's other side. Doesn't touch him. Just exists — like she always does on the mat.

Wide shot: **seven kids + one father** in a hallway that feels too narrow and too big.

No one fills the silence wrong.

**[HAIKYUU — team hallway wait]**

**[END SCENE 2]**

---

### SCENE 3 — ROOM 412 · NIGHT
**Panels:** (lead-in to 59.1)

**ACTION:**  
Ethan alone. Door closed. Pulls curtain himself.

Halmeoni small in the bed. Oxygen nasal line. Monitors — soft beep, soft beep.

She turns her head. Finds him.

**HALMEONI**  
*(Korean, weak)*  
Eun-ha… you came.

**ETHAN**  
*(Korean — careful pronunciation)*  
Ne, halmeoni. I'm here.

**HALMEONI**  
Training?

**ETHAN**  
Yes.

**HALMEONI**  
Good feet?

**ETHAN**  
*(almost laugh)*  
MV says so.

**HALMEONI**  
*(touches his cheek — cold hand, warm intent)*  
My champion. Not when you win. When you come back.

**ETHAN**  
*(can't hold it — voice cracks)*  
I'm not leaving.

**HALMEONI**  
*(faint smile)*  
You already did. To that good school. That's okay.

Beat. Monitor rhythm. Her eyes heavy.

**HALMEONI**  
The loud boy. Logan?

**ETHAN**  
He's outside. They all are.

**HALMEONI**  
Tell them… ramen is not dinner. But eat anyway.

**ETHAN**  
*(choking)*  
Okay.

**ACTION:**  
Her grip on his hand loosens — not yet gone. Ethan presses forehead to blanket edge. Shoulders shake — silent.

**FADE TO BLACK.**

**SFX:** *(monitor tone — flat, steady — then cut to silence)*

**[END CHAPTER 58]**

# CHAPTER 59 — WHAT'S LEFT

---

### SCENE 4 — ROOM 412 · DAWN
**Panels:** 59.1

**ACTION:**  
Same room. Grey dawn. Curtain open.

Ethan's still in the chair. Same clothes. Eyes red, dry.

Halmeoni's hand in his. Still.

Nurse enters — professional gentleness. Checks monitor. Looks at Ethan.

**NURSE**  
Take your time. There's no rush now.

She leaves a small box of tissues and exits.

Ethan doesn't speak to the room. Whispers to her — English, then Korean — fragments:

**ETHAN**  
I'll… I'll still…  
*(Korean)*  
Saranghae. I'm sorry I was late sometimes.

He lays his **Kwon's academy patch** from his bag on the blanket — folded neat.

Stands. Legs numb. Bows — deep, held — student to someone who taught him before any coach.

**[END SCENE 4]**

---

### SCENE 5 — HALLWAY · MORNING
**Panels:** 59.1 cont.

**ACTION:**  
Door opens.

Team still there. Some slept sitting. Logan's head on TJ's shoulder. Kieryn's mascara smudged — she'll deny it forever. Kian stares at floor. Ariana and Mr. Yang — caldereta empty, washed in sink down hall.

No one asks *how*.

Ethan walks into the cluster. Logan stands — hug — Ethan doesn't lift arms at first — then does — brief, crushing.

**TJ**  
*(quiet)*  
We’re here.

**KIERRYN**  
*(to no one)*  
Yeah. We are.

**KIAN**  
*(not performing)*  
My halmeoni died when I was nine. I didn't tell anyone for a week.  
*(beat)*  
Don't do that.

Ethan looks at him — first real eye contact.

**ETHAN**  
Okay.

**MR. YANG**  
*(puts hand on Ethan's head — blessing weight)*  
God keep her. You keep training. She would want both.

Ariana nods once — agreement without words.

**MV**  
*(arrives — coat, coffee he won't drink)*  
Car's outside. Everyone sleeps today. Ethan — you too.

**ETHAN**  
I have—

**MV**  
Not a request.

**[HAIKYUU — silent support, no fixing]**

**[END CHAPTER 59 — DEATH SEQUENCE]**

### SCENE 6 — KWON'S DOJANG · THREE DAYS LATER · EVENING
**Panels:** 59.2 · 59.3

**ACTION:**  
Ethan back on mats. Didn't want to be. MV said *move or drown*.

Light sparring — Ethan vs **Kian**. Controlled pace. Few students watching from edges.

**MV**  
Light contact. Headgear on. Breathe.

**ACTION:**  
Kian feints — sells hard — **roundhouse to head** — pulled but still **tags** — electronic **BEEP** on practice gear.

Something **snaps**.

Ethan's eyes — pupils sharp, empty.

Guard **drops**.

He **rushes** — not sparring anymore — **fury** — kicks blur — speed wrong — **no blocking** — Kian stumbles back — actually scared.

**KIAN**  
Whoa— ETHAN—

**ETHAN**  
*(not words — animal sound)*

Third kick — Kian catches forearm on hogu — still **knocks him down**.

MV **sprints** between them — no formality — **bear-hug from behind** — stops Ethan like a wall.

**MV**  
**STOP.** Ethan. **STOP.**

Ethan thrashes once — twice — goes limp.

Silence. Ethan panting. Hands shaking. Doesn't know where he is.

**LOGAN**  
*(from sideline, scared)*  
Hyung…?

**KIERRYN**  
*(whisper)*  
What *was* that?

**ACTION:**  
MV eases Ethan to sitting. Kian on floor — propped on elbows — blood lip — staring.

**KIAN**  
*(not joking)*  
I've seen guys get angry. That's not angry. That's… gone.

Ethan looks at his hands like they belong to someone else.

**MV**  
*(kneels — eye level — firm)*  
Grief is not a technique. You don't get to keep fighting like that without paying for it.

**ETHAN**  
*(barely)*  
She's gone and I still—

**MV**  
I know.  
*(beat)*  
You come back tomorrow. We name it. We learn the trigger. You are not allowed to hurt your family on this mat.

**ETHAN**  
*(looks at Kian)*  
I'm sorry.

**KIAN**  
*(long beat — wipes lip)*  
Don't apologize to me. Learn it. Because if you do that to someone who doesn't love you, they'll break you.

**TJ**  
*(helps Kian up)*  
He'll learn.

**MV**  
*(to team)*  
Everyone out. Ethan — ten minutes. Then we talk.

**ACTION:**  
Team files out. Logan last — looks back — Ethan small on mat — gold headphones around neck from someone's bag — borrowed comfort.

**MV (V.O.)** over Ethan alone:

**MV (V.O.)**  
Speed. Power. No guard. That's not a gift. That's a storm.  
*(beat)*  
Storms flood houses. We build levees — or we drown.

**[TEACH combat — Berserker Rage named next session, not this chapter]**

**VISUAL NOTE:**  
First berserker: desaturated background except red edge on Ethan's silhouette. After MV stops him — color returns.

**[END CHAPTER 59]**
