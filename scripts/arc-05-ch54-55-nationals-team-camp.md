Lower two inches. He's not Ethan at Prime — he's **here**, and we **coach**.

**ETHAN (V.O.)**

*(quiet, to Ethan)*  
You good?

**ETHAN**

# CHAPTER 55 — SPAR ROTATION & NIGHT WALK

**Panels:** 55.1

Morning — rotation stations. **MV periodization** on whiteboard.

Ethan vs Kian — Ethan wins round 1 with cut kick **+2** setup — Kian wins round 2 with feint-whirlwind.
