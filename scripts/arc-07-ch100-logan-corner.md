### SCENE 3 — AFTER ARIANA [HAIKYUU]
**Panels:** 100.6

Hallway — hours after Ch 94. Ariana re-wrapping bandages. Logan **kicks a plastic chair** — not at anyone — at the air.

**LOGAN**  
I wrote **plant after nine**. I **said** it.

**Ariana**  
I heard you.

**LOGAN**  
…

**Ariana**  
*(doesn't look up)*  
I chose the tenth anyway. That's on **me**. Not your note.

Beat. Logan nods — doesn't know if that's forgiveness or fact.

### SCENE 4 — AFTER ETHAN [HAIKYUU]
**Panels:** 100.7

Night after Ch 98. Ethan sitting on hallway floor — gear half off — not broken — **empty**.

Logan sits beside him. Doesn't hug. **Fist bump.**

**LOGAN**  
You **read** it. You still lost.

**ETHAN**  
Yeah. And I'm still standing.

Logan **nods** — enough.

**LOGAN (internal)**  
Kieryn found it on tape. I saw it live. He landed it anyway.  
Losing can still be **growth**. Ttong Kim never taught me that word.
