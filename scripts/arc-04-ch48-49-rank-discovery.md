# CHAPTER 47 — RUMOR [REAL #16]

---

### SCENE 1 — FOOD COURT
**Panels:** 47.1

**ACTION:**  
Neutral ground. Former Prime classmate — not hostile — tired.

**FRIEND**  
Did you hear? He's not even a real **master**.

Ethan stirs ramen — doesn't react yet.

**FRIEND**  
Like — legally. Dan rank. I looked it up for a joke and now I can't unsee it.

**ETHAN**  
*(flat)*  
What did you find?

**FRIEND**  
I'll send the link. Don't start a war. I just thought you should know you're not crazy.

**[END SCENE 1]**

---

# CHAPTER 48 — RESEARCH [REAL #17–18]

---

### SCENE 2 — LAPTOP
**Panels:** 48.1

**ACTION:**  
Ethan's room — credential database — official registry.

**ON SCREEN:**  
**Kim, Ttong — 2nd Dan — Instructor Level — Prime Taekwondo Sports Dynasty**

Ethan stares. Scrolls again. Same.

**ETHAN (V.O.)**  
Second dan. Instructor. Not master.

His own profile tab open from Kwon's paperwork:

**Hyun, Ethan — 3rd Dan**

Silence. Then — breath hitches — **laughter** — can't stop — tears different from Arc 3.

**ETHAN**  
*(to empty room)*  
I'm **third dan**. I **outrank** him.

**[END SCENE 2]**

---

# CHAPTER 49 — COMEDY CHAPTER [HAIKYUU]

---

### SCENE 3 — DOJANG FLOOR
**Panels:** 49.1 · 49.2

**ACTION:**  
Ethan tells TJ first — whisper — then whole team.

**TJ**  
*(processing)*  
Wait. Wait wait wait—

**TJ**  
*(drops to mat — wheezing laugh)*  
He called **you** too soft?!

**KIERYN**  
*(deadpan)*  
That explains the gear obsession.

**KIAN**  
Impossible.  
*(beat)*  
Actually no. That tracks.

**LOGAN**  
So Ethan could've… ordered him around?

**ETHAN**  
*(still laughing)*  
Technically on paper—

**MV**  
*(from office door)*  
If you're done auditing the universe, water break. Then footwork.

Team scatters — still giggling.

**Ariana** — corner of mouth almost smiles — returns to stretching — nobody comments — she keeps the almost.

**[END SCENE 3]**

---

# CHAPTER 50 — LETTING THE SHADOW EXIST

---

### SCENE 4 — MV WISDOM
**Panels:** 50.1

**ACTION:**  
MV and Ethan alone — after class.

**MV**  
You verified rank. Good. Now don't confuse paperwork with permission to be cruel.

**ETHAN**  
I'm not—

**MV**  
I know.  
*(beat)*  
**Rank is paperwork. Character is what you do next.**

**ETHAN**  
He still messaged my grandmother's hospital week.

**MV**  
Then you delete. You train. You don't become him to beat him.

**[END SCENE 4]**

---

### SCENE 5 — LATE NIGHT MAT
**Panels:** 50.2

**ACTION:**  
Ethan alone — lights low — roundhouse into shield — again — again.

Last threatening voicemail — **delete**.

First time in the series — **small smile** while kicking.

**ETHAN (V.O.)**  
Third dan. Still scared sometimes. Not of his belt anymore.

**[END ARC 4 BEAT]**
