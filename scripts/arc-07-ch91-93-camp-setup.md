#### ARIANA — **Whirling Dervish**
**Station:** Body-head pad tree — **Ariana** alone — team watches from doorway.

**MV**  
Ten hits. Body and head. **Three kicks** — then **foot down**. That's WT. Do it again until you hit ten. Don't stop until I say stop.

**Ariana** — no nod — enters.

**Hits 1–3** — punch **+1** body, cut **+2** trunk, clinch kick — spins link strike to strike — **plants on three**.  
**Hits 4–6** — roundhouse head, cut body, punch — **no plant until six** — **foot down**.  
**Hits 7–9** — head, head, body — breathing steady — **plant on nine**.  
**Hit 10** — final cut — **foot down** — **guard up** — stops **exactly** on MV's whistle.

Silence. Then **Kieryn** claps once.

**Ariana**  
*(barely audible)*  
**Whirling Dervish.** Ten hits. **Three — then down.**

**MV**  
Logged. **Legal** in a real bout — **three kicks**, then you **plant**. Four in the air and the ref stops you. **After nine you still plant before ten.** Ten is not a free kick. Endurance plus accuracy. Not chaos.

**ETHAN**  
…Ten?

**Ariana**  
You count when you **corner** me.

**[TEACH — Whirling Dervish: alternating body/head with spins; **max 3 consecutive kicks** before foot must contact mat — 10-hit chain = 3+3+3+1 with plants between groups; 4+ without planting = illegal]**
