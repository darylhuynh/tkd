# CHAPTER 1 — NIGHTMARE

---

### SCENE 1 — TOURNAMENT VENUE · 7:04 AM
**Panels:** 1.1 · 1.2

**ACTION:**  
Exterior — *Premier Taekwondo Sports Dynasty — Regional Open*. Overcast. Fluorescents inside.

Kids in full hogus shuffle in. Clock: **7:04 AM**.

**KID A**  
Why are we here so early?

No answer from staff.

**[END SCENE 1]**

---

### SCENE 2 — THE WAIT
**Panels:** 1.3 · 1.4 · 1.5 · 1.6 · 1.7

**ACTION:**  
Bracket board: **Hyun, Ethan — −45kg — Ring 4 — 3:12 PM**.

Ethan's finger on his name. Internal clock starts.

**ETHAN (V.O.)**  
Seven hours.

Wide shot: twenty-plus Premier kids in gear. **Ttong Kim** at far end — coffee with parents — **Repeul Kim** at the edge of the cluster, arms folded, sour face — silent.

No mats in use. No warmup. No instructor eyes on fighters.

Ethan alone on bleachers. Empty seats both sides. Mouthguard case open. Legs in hogu.

Montage — same seat:
- Phone: **10:23 AM** — vending wrapper  
- Clock: **1:47 PM** — other kids on phones — Ethan staring at distant ring  

**ETHAN (V.O.)**  
Same dobok. Same patch.

**[END SCENE 2]**

---

### SCENE 3 — PAID COACHING
**Panels:** 1.8 · 1.9 · 1.10 · 1.11

**ACTION:**  
Quick cut — check to **Ttong Kim**. VIP brochure. **Repeul Kim** behind him with brochure stack — blank mean stare.

**TTONG KIM**  
We'll take good care of him.

Cut — he kneels — adjusts **another kid's** headgear — laughing. Parents proud.

Ethan's POV — far away — backs turned.

**PARENT (O.S.)**  
Loosen up, kid.

Random parent **yanks Ethan's arm** into a bad stretch. Ethan winces. Instructor doesn't look.

Flash inset: dojang scrimmage — Ethan lands clean head kick — smirks.

Present: phone **2:47 PM**. Alone.

**ETHAN (V.O.)**  
Won every scrimmage last month.

**[END SCENE 3]**

### SCENE 5 — KO BOUNTY [REAL #28]
**Panels:** 1.13b

**ACTION:**  
Staging area. **Ttong Kim** holds **1,000 won** up for Premier black belts.

**Repeul Kim** blurred at his shoulder — multicolor disaster — scowl.

**TTONG KIM**  
One thousand won if you knock them out. Black belt doesn't mean nice — it means you finish them.

Eyes find Ethan.

**TTONG KIM**  
*(lower)*  
Too soft. Too nice to be a real black belt.

Ethan's jaw tight. Doesn't take the coin.

**[END SCENE 5]**

---

### SCENE 6 — GO FIGHT [REAL #13]
**Panels:** 1.14 · 1.15

**ACTION:**  
First time all day **Ttong Kim** stands close to Ethan. Glances at watch. Points at ring.

**TTONG KIM**  
Go fight.

No name. No plan. No *how's your body after eight hours*.

Ethan steps in **cold** — stiff legs — corner empty — instructor already turned to next paying family.

**[END SCENE 6]**

---

### SCENE 7 — THE KICK
**Panels:** 1.15 · 1.16

**ACTION:**  
Ring. Opponent older — feint — **spinning hook kick** — head sensor **BEEP**.

Ethan's legs buckle. Sound **drops out**.

Background: **Ttong Kim** — disappointed. Not concerned.

**SFX:** *(silence)*

**[END SCENE 7]**

---

### SCENE 8 — WAKE UP
**Panels:** 1.16 cont.

**ACTION:**  
**SMASH CUT** — Ethan jolts upright in bed. Sweat. Gasping.

Dawn light. Gold headphones on nightstand.

Hand raised — blocking a kick that isn't there.

**ETHAN (V.O.)**  
*(breathing)*  
Not again.

**FADE OUT.**

**[END CHAPTER 1]**
