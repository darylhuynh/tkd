### SCENE 3 — BLEACHERS DISSOCIATION ★
**Panels:** 25.11 · 26–30 content **intercut here** · 31.1

**ACTION — PRESENT:**

Ethan alone on bleachers. Gear half off. Arena blur.

**DISSOCIATION MONTAGE — woven while he sits** *(reader experiences flash as Ethan's memory surfacing)*

**[26 — Warm opening]**  
White belt. Small dojang. **Ttong Kim (younger):** *Welcome to the family.*

**[27 — Lies REAL #8]**  
Crowded dojang. Parents after tourney.  
**Ttong Kim:** *They bought those belts. Your kids earned theirs.*

**[28 — Board REAL #24]** — full beats inline:

Ethan smallest at board line. Board **too high**. *Thud. Thud. Thud.* — wrong fist line — board flexes, won't snap.

**PARENT C (O.S. — whisper)**  
Why don't they **move it closer**? He's too short for that height—

**TTONG KIM**  
*(to another parent — not Ethan)*  
Good power. Needs commitment.

He does **not** look at Ethan's feet. Does **not** lower the board. **Says nothing.**

**MOTHER (O.S.)**  
Can he stop? He already—

**TTONG KIM**  
He'll break it or he won't. Sit down.

**TTONG KIM**  
Then let him learn what quitting looks like.

**Almost an hour.** Ethan **shifts one foot forward** — adjusts hip — **finally** lines fist with center — **alone**.

Board **splits** — not heroic — swollen hand.

**TTONG KIM (quiet disgust)**  
Late and ugly isn't the same as good. **One out of five.**

Parents **crying** — **whispering after the break**:

**PARENT D (O.S.)**  
He wasn't punching the **right spot** until he stepped in. That's when it broke.

**PARENT E (O.S.)**  
The instructor should've said **one word**. Adjust. Step. **Anything**.

Results: Ethan **highest pass** — Ha-eun **bonus** — Ethan **#2**.

**TTONG KIM**  
First place — Ha-eun. Second — Ethan. Both pass.

**ETHAN (V.O. — flash)**  
It broke when I **moved**. Not when they **helped**. I would have passed with zero. Nothing left but fear.

**[29 — Go fight REAL #13]**  
Exhibition ring. Points. **Go fight.**

**[30 — Head kick + scold REAL #11–12]**  
Same hook. Guard drops. Twenty-minute scold. Tears.

**PRESENT SNAPS BACK — STYLE: TOURNAMENT / HAIKYUU:**

**Logan** sits beside Ethan — no words — shoulders touch.

**TJ** crouches — eye level.

**TJ**  
That kick didn't win him the war. Next ring is ours.

Ethan blinks. Present returns — slow.

**MV (optional — distant corner, to assistant)**  
Same kick. He just didn't know its name yet. Now he does.

**[END CHAPTER 31]**
