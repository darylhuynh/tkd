## Usage Notes for AI Storyboarding

When generating panels for **[REAL]** scenes:

1. **Preserve exact dialogue** where quoted above — these lines carry autobiographical weight.
2. **Premier scenes:** colder palette, crowded mat, instructor's back turned on Ethan.
3. **Kwon's scenes:** warmer light, MV facing students, eye contact, paddles and shields with feedback.
4. **Spinning hook kick trigger:** slow-motion head snap, electronic scoreboard flash, audio cut to silence before flashback.
5. **Board break [#24]:** clock on wall, bruised fist close-up, instructor's disappointed face in foreground, crowd blur with tissues — **no heroic breaking frame**; exhaustion not triumph.
6. **Girl favoritism [#9, #25–27]:** off-center framing — instructor facing girls, back to boys; whispers; **never depict explicit misconduct** — implication through **silence, bonus points, promotion chart, sleepover brag left uncorrected**.
7. **KO bounty [#28]:** coin/bill close-up — **1,000 won** — staging hall; **Ttong Kim's eyes on Ethan** when he says black belts must **knock out** opponents / Ethan is **too nice**; kids' reactions; **not** celebratory — contrast MV Arc 3 Ch 22.
