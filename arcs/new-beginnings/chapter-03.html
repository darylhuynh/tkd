Silver Belt

MV Approaches

No Sales Pitch

MV gestures to sign — *Kwon's Taekwondo Academy*. No trophy wall of lies. Simple, honest space.

MV: "No pressure. What's your name?"

Ethan Speaks

Ethan: "...Ethan."

MV: "Viet Na. Most people say Master Viet. Either's fine."

MV: "You looking or training?"

Training

*(none — ritual)*

3.13

Quiet Class

Observation — no one filming for social. No parents coaching from bleachers. Just training.

Ethan (internal): *No audience.*

MV: "That's a serious belt."

3.15

No Sales Pitch (Sign)

MV: "We don't sell dreams. We train bodies."

3.16

Ethan Almost Smiles

3.17

Other Students Resume

Class returns to drills — but side glances — curiosity not hostility.

Cadet (whisper): "He moves like a black belt."

3.18

MV Assigns Space

MV points open mat lane — not back corner exile.

MV: "You can work here. Same air as everyone else."

3.19

First Instruction

MV holds paddle low — testing reaction speed, not showmanship.

MV: "When I say hold, you hold. When I say go, you go. Clear?"

3.20

Ethan Nods

Ethan nods once — sharp. First crack of trust.

Ethan: "Clear."
